ABOUT THE CAVEMEN

The Cavemen are Nigerian highlife band consisting of sibling duo kingsley okorie,a bassist and benjamin james,a drummer.They were discoverd by lady donli and are known for their live performance.they won the 2020 Headies award for the alternative album.

  • Origin: Lagos
  • Genre: Highlife
  • Members: kingsley,Okorie,Benjamin James
  • Record Label: Ambious Record
  • Nominations: The Headis Award For Best Altanative Song
